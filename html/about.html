Your hosts are ...

Steve Becker - Network Engineer by day, musician and author by night. Steve self-published A Guide to the Recovery Toolbox and is working on an album, works-in-progress get uploaded to SoundCloud now and then. Steve has lived in Milwaukee, Chicago, Philadephia, and a number of places in NJ and FL, most notably Tech Duinn, Brigadoon, and Atlantis.

Chris was raised in the Florida Mountains by carnivorous gorillas. He's still getting the hang of silverware.
